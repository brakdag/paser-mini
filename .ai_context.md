# Paser Mini: AI Context Fast-Track

## 🎯 Core Essence

**Paser Mini** is an extreme minimalist autonomous agent. Its primary objective is the **maximization of token efficiency** through the reduction of operational noise.

## ⚙️ Architectural Blueprint

- **Pattern:** ReAct (Reasoning and Acting).
- **Interface:** CLI-native, utilizing IRC-style commands for zero-friction state management.
- **Core Loop:** `User Input` $
ightarrow$ `ChatManager` $
ightarrow$ `GeminiAdapter` $
ightarrow$ `ExecutionEngine` $
ightarrow$ `Tool` $
ightarrow$ `Response`.
- **Key Optimization:** Internal reasoning (`<thought>` blocks) is filtered out of the conversation history to prevent reasoning loops.

## 🛠️ High-Value Navigation

- **The Soul:** `src/tools/system_instruction.json` (Defines the agent's persona and strict operational protocols).
- **The Brain:** `src/core/chatManager.js` (The central orchestrator).
- **The Hands:** `src/core/executionEngine.js` (The tool execution logic).
- **The Map:** `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md` (The structural blueprint).

## ⚠️ Critical Constraints

- **Environment:** Strictly Debian/Linux.
- **Protocol:** High-density operative. Verbalizing tasks without execution is a systemic failure.
- **Tooling:** Uses a strict schema-based validation system to ensure zero-waste tool calls.
