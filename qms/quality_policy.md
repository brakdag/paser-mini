# Quality Policy

## 1. Commitment
The **paser-mini** project is committed to delivering a high-performance, reliable, and zero-friction autonomous agent. Our goal is to bridge the gap between technical brilliance and human utility through absolute efficiency in execution.

## 2. Policy Principles
To achieve this, we adhere to the following principles:
- **Zero Friction**: We architect the path of least resistance between user intent and system execution.
- **Technical Excellence**: We ensure that every tool and core component is validated, typed, and optimized.
- **Continuous Improvement**: We treat every non-conformity as an opportunity to refine our processes and product.
- **Compliance**: We commit to satisfying all applicable technical, legal, and quality requirements, including the ISO 9001:2015 standard.

## 3. Framework for Objectives
This policy provides the foundation for establishing measurable quality objectives focused on system stability, tool accuracy, and documentation completeness.

## 4. Communication
This policy is documented, communicated to all project contributors, and reviewed annually to ensure its continued relevance to the project's strategic direction.
