# Audit Plan - paser-mini

## Objective
Verify compliance of the paser-mini project with ISO 9001:2015 standards.

## Scope
All documented processes, architectural definitions, and operational protocols.

## Audit Criteria
- ISO 9001:2015 Clauses 4 through 10.
- Internal Documentation Policy.

## Methodology
1. Document Review: Analysis of existing .md files in /docs.
2. Gap Analysis: Comparison between ISO requirements and current documentation.
3. Non-Conformity Reporting: Logging of systemic failures in qms/non_conformities.md.
