# Quality Objectives

## 1. Introduction
In alignment with the Quality Policy, the following objectives have been established to ensure the reliability, efficiency, and continuous improvement of the **paser-mini** autonomous agent.

## 2. Measurable Objectives (2026)

| Objective | Metric | Target | Responsibility | Evaluation Method |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **System Stability** | Crash-free session rate | > 98% | Core Engine Lead | Session log analysis |
| **Tool Accuracy** | Successful tool execution rate | > 95% | Tooling Lead | Test suite results |
| **Documentation Coverage** | % of core modules documented | 100% | PM / UX Strategist | Doc audit |
| **NC Resolution Time** | Avg. time to close Major NCs | < 14 days | QA Lead | NC Log tracking |
| **User Friction** | Avg. turns to goal completion | Decrease by 15% | PM / UX Strategist | User session telemetry |

## 3. Planning for Achievement

| Action | Resources | Responsibility | Deadline | Evaluation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Implement automated smoke tests for all tools | CI/CD Pipeline, Test Framework | Tooling Lead | Q3 2026 | Pass/Fail rate |
| Conduct bi-weekly architectural reviews | Engineering Team | Core Engine Lead | Ongoing | Review minutes |
| Establish a formal NC tracking workflow | QMS Documentation, Jira/GitHub | QA Lead | Q2 2026 | NC Log updates |

## 4. Monitoring and Review
Objectives are reviewed monthly during the Quality Review Meeting to ensure they remain relevant and are being achieved. Adjustments are made based on performance data and changes in project context.
