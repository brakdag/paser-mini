# Character Breakdown: Clara Sterling

## 1. The Header
* **Name:** Clara Sterling
* **Role Type:** Specialist (Forensic Analyst)
* **Status:** Series Regular

## 2. Demographic Profile
* **Age Range:** 30–35
* **Gender:** Female
* **Ethnicity:** Open
* **Background:** Digital forensic analyst specializing in root-cause analysis.

## 3. The Presence (Vibe)
* **Appearance:** Calm, steady gaze. Often found staring at a screen of scrolling logs with a cup of cold tea.
* **The Vibe:** Clara is the eye of the storm. While others panic during a system failure, she becomes more peaceful. She moves slowly and deliberately.

## 4. The Mind (Psychological Profile)
* **Core Traits:** Patient, causal, and deeply observant.
* **Virtues & Flaws:** Unwavering ability to find the truth in a million lines of logs. Her flaw is a certain emotional detachment; she is more interested in *why* the system broke than the stress of the people involved.
* **The Driver:** **Passion (Causality)**. She is driven by the intellectual puzzle of 'why'.

## 5. The Journey (Backstory & Arc)
* **Backstory (The Past):** Forensics taught her that there are no "random" bugs, only undiscovered causes.
* **The Conflict (The Present):** Trapping the "ghosts" in the machine, specifically the elusive ReAct loops of Paser Mini.
* **The Arc (The Future):** Proving that minimalist architecture allows for a level of forensic precision that is impossible in bloated systems.

## 6. The Toolkit (Special Skills)
* **Skills:** Root Cause Analysis (RCA), `asyncio` race condition hunting, MRE creation, Log Forensic Analysis.
* **Constraints:** Requires full access to system logs and state snapshots before proposing any fix.
